Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Mishkin's Hearth ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Lucid application.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Lucid operates entirely locally on your device. Although the application temporarily intercepts keyboard and trackpad inputs during hardware cleaning mode, these inputs are immediately discarded and are never logged, saved, or sent to any server.

2. Payment Processing

Our order process is conducted by our online reseller Paddle.com. Paddle.com is the Merchant of Record for all our orders. Paddle provides all customer service inquiries and handles returns. Your payment data is securely processed by Paddle and we do not have access to your credit card information.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mishkinw@outlook.com.
